Hello! Welcome to my portfolio! I am born in South Korea but was raised in Vancouver, Canada. I began working on my portfolio summer 2019 when I took time to explore my interests. After talking to experienced designers and having taken Intro to Product Design class in my freshman year, I realized that Product Design was exactly why I came to Cornell University - to work with people and technology.


I applied to my major, having no clue on what to become but knowing that I wanted to work in an area where technology and people were interconnected. I’ve always believed technology excited solely for the people and people, as I have, are also benefited by technology interchangeably. I know I have a lot to learn from others and from my surroundings, and I have been loving it so far. I hope to one day be able to make a greater social impact using my technical skills, creativity, and empathy!
