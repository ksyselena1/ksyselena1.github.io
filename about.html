About Me

Tech is changing our world, but without users, tech is nothing.

Welcome to my portfolio! I've always been changing and adapting: to different countries, schools, sports, hobbies, instruments, etc. As you can see in my portfolio, I explored various areas such as photography, product design, data analytics, and web development. I loved working in all these aspects and trying to discover where my passion lies.

What I've come to realize overall: I love improving user experience.

As stated in my "thought" in the first line, I recognize that tech is futile without users. Therefore, I want to work for the users. Although there are so many different ways of doing this, for instance through web development, data analytics, user testing, and more, one in particular has earned my interest and trust the most: data.

The past explains the present and the present drives the future.

Data comes from the users and is able to show the past, present, and forecast the future. The ever-increasing data is a quantifiable, observable metric that has so much potential and power for improving user experience.

I am currently looking for a position where I can work with data, interpreting and analyzing quantitative metrics to improve user experience.
